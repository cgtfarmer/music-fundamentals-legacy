The Fundamentals of Music

The purpose of this site is to share my understanding of the fundamentals of music theory.

This site should be considered a "living document" such that it will always be a "work in progress" to some extent, and edits over time will be made to improve the explanations, content organization, etc. to more efficiently confer understanding to the visitor.

Currently, the "flow" of the explanations assumes the reader visits each page in a linear fashion, from left to right, and then going from top to bottom. Thus, the first page to visit is the page describing what I refer to as, "The 3 Pillars of Musical Ability" to get a "top-down", "overview" kind of understanding about where "all the pieces fit in the big picture". Then the "Music Theory" page has 3 sections that give you 90% of what you need to know without understanding too much of the "how it works" (General, Modes/Scales, Chords/The NNS), and then one pretty huge section that gets pretty detailed (Intervals - Scale/Chord Qualities) and explains the rest
